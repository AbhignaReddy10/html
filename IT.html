Information Technology in IARE

The Department of Information Technology (IT) was established in the year 2001 with the B.Tech program in Information Technology, initially with an intake of 120. Over the years, the increasing global demand for IT professionals and the rapid expansion of digital technologies have driven the department to grow and diversify its academic offerings. In line with industry trends and emerging technological domains, the department introduced the B.Tech program in Computer Science and Information Technology (CSIT) in the year 2020 with an intake of 180.



Evolution of IT

The B.Tech program in Information Technology has been accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for five consecutive terms in 2008, 2013, 2016, 2019, and 2023.
